Load channels using URL


Copy and paste a M3U playlist link in the text box of Settings UI, then tap on the "Load" button to load channels.


You should always ask your IPTV provider for playlist files.


For testing purpose only, you can also use one of the following links to load channles: (Please remove the channels from the App once you finished testing)


https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/index.m3u

https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/channels.json
